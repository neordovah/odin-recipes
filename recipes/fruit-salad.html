Fruit salad

Back to the main page

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Pineapple
  • Blueberries
  • Red grapes
  • Mandarin oranges
  • Bananas
  • Honey
  • Limes

Preparation

  1. Chop the fruits and add to a large mixing bowl.
  2. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together the honey, lime zest and lime juice.
  3. Pour dressing over fruit just before serving and toss to evenly coat.
  4. Enjoy!
